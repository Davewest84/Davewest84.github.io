📝 Booking Notes

Village Hotels pool surcharge: All three Village Hotels charge ~£5/room/night extra for pool access. Worth it for the bigger pools and better facilities (jacuzzi, steam, sauna).

Holiday Inn kids perks: Both Holiday Inns offer kids under 18 staying free and under 13 eating free — makes them significantly cheaper for a family of 4.

Best for a morning activity: Telford (Ironbridge Gorge), Village Chester (Chester Zoo), Ellesmere Port (Cheshire Oaks shopping).

If heading south: Holiday Inn Bromsgrove is the furthest from Llangollen but puts you furthest along the route home — worth considering if you want to do most of the driving on day 1.

City centre parking: Most city hotels charge £18–20/day for parking. Factor this in when comparing to the suburban options with free parking.

Novotel family deal: The Novotel Liverpool is exceptional value — 2 kids under 16 stay free, eat breakfast free, get a welcome gift, and you get 50% off a second room. Hard to beat for a family of 4.

Castlefield hidden gem: The Castlefield Hotel gives free access to the Y Club next door (18m pool, sauna, steam, basketball) and the Science & Industry Museum is literally next door and free. Great combo for kids.
